Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

When you submit the contact form on this site, we collect the information you provide: your name, email address, phone number, company name, location, and the contents of your message. We do not collect data through cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics tools beyond standard web hosting logs.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for any purpose. Your information will not be used for marketing or added to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

Data Retention

Inquiry records are retained for internal record-keeping purposes related to our acquisition activities. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us directly through the form on this site.

Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Contact

To request deletion of your data or with any privacy questions, contact us through the form on the Connect page.
